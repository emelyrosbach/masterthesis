Round 1 - Application of Artificial Intelligence in Digital Pathology

Thank you for taking this 20 minute survey! The goal of this study is to gain insights into the employment of artificial intelligence in digital pathology and shine a light onto the cooperation of human and machine in terms of efficiency, acceptance and usefulness. Additionally, we seek to examine the influence of AI and a high-stress setting on inter-observer variability. As part of this investigation, your assignment will involve the estimation of the tumor cell percentage based on the provided H&E stained slides. This study consists of two rounds, which will be held within a two week interval, and involves a total of 18 slides per run, with nine of them having to be rated under the constraint of a timer. By presing the button below, you will start round 1 of the experiment

Even though this study is completely voluntary and not compensated monetarily, we would like to show our appriciation for your participation by sending you a summary of the study findings weeks before they will be published officially, so please be sure to enter your e-mail address in the text field below.
